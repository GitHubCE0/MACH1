About MACH1

MACH1 stands as a forefront design and manufacturing entity, distinguished for its proficiency in the comprehensive spectrum of design, manufacturing, and serial production of composite components, assemblies, and systems.

See Gallery

We offer a comprehensive suite of services encompassing design solutions, tooling design, DFM, and series production of standard and custom composite components for Automotive, Aerospace, Marine, and UAV applications.

Services

MACH1 provides an extensive array of professional services, catering to a diverse range of client needs.

Manufacturing And Production

We specialize in manufacturing and series production of prototypes, standard, and custom parts, ensuring precision and quality in every aspect of the process.

Consultation services

Expertise in design includes component and tooling design, GD&T, drawing creation, DFX, reverse engineering, FMEA, and risk analysis. Our manufacturing capabilities encompass DFM, PFMEA, cost reduction, production planning, and plant layout.

CAD Modeling

Our services cover CAD modeling, drawing creation, assembly, and thorough clash checks to guarantee precise and error-free designs for seamless project execution.

Supply Chain and Product Managment

Our services extend to supporting and establishing processes for supply chain and program management, ensuring efficient operations and successful project execution.

Testing & Qualification

We conduct NDT, DT, and validation tests on various parts and prototypes, ensuring the highest quality and compliance with industry standards.

Product/Component Lifecycle Solutions

From conceptualization to realization, we specialize in the comprehensive development of products and components for aerospace, UAV, automotive, marine, and diverse applications, ensuring precision and innovation throughout the process.
